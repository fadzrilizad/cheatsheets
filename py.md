### Math Operators

- `+` Addition
- `-` Subtraction
- `*` Multiplication
- `/` Division
- `**` Exponention
- `%` Modulo
- `//` Integer division
- PEMDAS order of operation
  - Parentheses
  - Exponents
  - Multiplication
  - Division
  - Addition
  - Subtraction
- `num ** 0.5 = sqrt num`
